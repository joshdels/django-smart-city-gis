Digital Transformation Roadmap

  1. Paper-Based

    Paper maps, printed records, manual filing, no digital workflows.

  2. CAD-Based

    AutoCAD drafting, isolated DWG files, no GIS database, manual updates.

  3. Survey & Technical Descriptions

    Relies on bearings, BLMS/LMB records, technical descriptions, manual parcel plotting.

  4. Desktop GIS

    Uses QGIS or ArcGIS Desktop, digital parcel layers, but limited automation and data sharing.

  5. GIS Data Management

    GIS-ready parcels, cleaned datasets, centralized spatial database (e.g., PostGIS, GeoServer, ArcGIS Online), standardized data.

  6. Web GIS

    Interactive web maps, online parcel search, multi-user collaboration, role-based access.

  7. Enterprise GIS

    GIS integrated with Assessor, Planning, Engineering, Zoning, Tax Mapping, permitting, and other departments.

  8. Smart Land Information System

    Automated workflows, dashboards, analytics, interoperability, decision support, and digital governance.
